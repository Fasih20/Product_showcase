# ADR-001: Establish Initial Architectural Baseline

**Status:** proposed

## Context

The recent codebase scan indicates the initial establishment of an architectural baseline. This is a foundational step for understanding and managing the evolution of the codebase. As no major hotspots have been identified, this represents a deliberate effort to define the starting point of our architectural documentation.

## Decision

The current state of the codebase, as captured by the initial scan, is formally recognized as the architectural baseline. This baseline will serve as the reference point for all future architectural analysis and decision-making.

## Consequences

- Provides a clear starting point for tracking architectural drift and evolution.
- Enables consistent application of architectural principles and standards moving forward.
- Facilitates communication about the codebase's architecture to new team members.
- Potential for the baseline to become outdated if not regularly reviewed and updated.

---

Related tasks: Schedule regular reviews of the architectural baseline., Define a process for updating the architectural baseline., Document the key architectural components and their relationships based on the current baseline.
